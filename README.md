### 📂 Featured Projects

#### 🌾 [DesiKit – Farm-to-Family Mobile PWA](https://github.com/ISHURAO)
> A high-performance, fully installable PWA connecting local farmers with consumers.
* **Problem Solved**: Transparent logistics and fair pricing.
* **Key Features**: Live Socket.io logistics tracking with Leaflet interactive maps, SMTP OTP pipelines, fuzzy regex-indexed search query matching.
* **Stack**: `React Native (PWA)`, `ReactJS`, `Node.js`, `MongoDB`, `Socket.io`, `Express.js`, `Leaflet`

#### 🍔 [Dabba Drop – Food Delivery Platform](https://github.com/ISHURAO/Dabba-Drop)
> Production-grade food delivery platform with role-based coordination.
* **Problem Solved**: High operational latency in local delivery pipelines.
* **Key Features**: GPS-based live order tracking with interactive maps, secure JWT authentication with OTP login, Razorpay integration.
* **Stack**: `React`, `Redux Toolkit`, `Node.js`, `Express.js`, `MongoDB`, `Socket.io`, `Razorpay`, `Cloudinary`

#### 🏘️ [GramSewa – Smart Panchayat](https://github.com/ISHURAO/Gram-Sewa)
> Digital governance platform for Indian Gram Panchayats. Manage services, complaints, and citizen records online.
* **Problem Solved**: Citizens traveling long distances for village administrative services.
* **Key Features**: Complaints portal, Sarpanch dashboard, Hindi i18n support, PWA for offline connectivity.
* **Stack**: `React`, `Node.js`, `MongoDB`, `Express`, `Tailwind`, `PWA`

#### 📧 [Email Management System](https://github.com/ISHURAO/Mail_Dashboard)
> Full-featured email dashboard with Inbox, Sent, Drafts, and complete CRUD operations.
* **Problem Solved**: Centralized local messaging routing.
* **Key Features**: Full CRUD routing, search/filter with pagination, responsive dashboard UI.
* **Stack**: `PHP`, `MySQL`, `HTML5`, `CSS3`, `JavaScript`

#### 🤖 [Online Learning Management System](https://github.com/ISHURAO/Online-Learning-Management-System)
> Comprehensive platform for managing online courses, assignments, and student progress.
* **Problem Solved**: Streamlining virtual learning and course tracking.
* **Key Features**: Course management (videos/quizzes), assessments with instant results, admin dashboard.
* **Stack**: `HTML5`, `CSS3`, `JavaScript`, `Tailwind`, `PHP`, `MySQL`

#### 🤖 [AI Resume Analyzer (ATS Scoring Tool)](https://github.com/ISHURAO)
> OpenAI-powered applicant tracking system analyzer.
* **Problem Solved**: High rejection rates due to non-optimized resumes.
* **Key Features**: OpenAI skill-gap analysis, resume scoring, keyword improvement suggestions.
* **Stack**: `React`, `Node.js`, `OpenAI API`, `Tailwind`
